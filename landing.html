FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to set up Twilio or any technical integrations myself? +
No. We handle everything. Your dedicated SMS business line is set up by our team. Your Facebook and Google lead forms get connected for you. You just log in and start working leads. Most customers are live within one business day.
Can my existing customers text my new business number? +
Yes. Your Service Job Pro business number is a real phone number that sends and receives texts like any other. You can forward calls to your existing phone, and texts are managed through the Service Job Pro app. Many customers also port their existing business number to us so their clients see the same number they always have.
How does the AI follow-up work exactly? +
When a new lead comes in — from Facebook, Google, your website, or a missed call — the system can automatically send a personalized text message using the lead's name, the service they requested, and how they found you. You can also use AI Draft to write a message in seconds and review it before sending. All AI messages are powered by Claude AI and sound natural, not robotic.
What home service businesses does this work best for? +
Service Job Pro works for any home service business that generates leads and books jobs — roofing, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, lawn care, landscaping, pest control, painting, flooring, windows, gutters, and more. If you get leads and book jobs, this system is built for you.
What happens after my 30-day free trial? +
At the end of your trial, you choose a plan and your account continues without interruption. If you decide not to continue, your account pauses — we never delete your data. All your leads, clients, automations, and settings are preserved if you return later. No surprises.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes, absolutely. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel any time from your account settings. We think you'll stay because the system delivers results — not because you're locked in.
